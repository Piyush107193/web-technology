University of Petroleum and Energy Studies

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is a premier institution in India known for its specialized focus on energy and petroleum sectors. Established in 2003, UPES has emerged as a leading educational hub, offering a range of programs in fields such as engineering, business, law, design, and more, all tailored to meet the dynamic demands of the energy industry. With a commitment to providing industry-relevant education, UPES boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections. The university's unique approach integrates academic excellence with practical exposure, preparing students to excel in their chosen professions. UPES not only imparts knowledge but also fosters innovation and research, contributing significantly to advancements in the energy sector. As a beacon of education in specialized domains, UPES continues to shape future leaders and professionals in the energy and allied industries.
